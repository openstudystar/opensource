Comprehension



Answer the following question:



a. Why did the speaker receive thousands of good-wishes cards and gifts from all over the world?

=> The speaker received thousands of good-wishes cards and gifts from around the world because people admired her courage and wanted to support her recovery after she was shot by the Taliban.



b. According to the speaker, what are hundreds of human rights activists and social workers struggling for?

=> The speaker states that hundreds of human rights activists and social workers are striving to achieve their goals of education, peace, and equality.



c. What has she learnt from Gandhi?

She learned the value of nonviolence from Gandhi.



d. In what sense is peace necessary for education?

=> Peace is necessary for education because it creates a safe and supportive environment for children to learn. In a peaceful setting, students are not afraid to attend school, and they can concentrate better on their studies. Without peace, fear and instability disrupt their ability to focus and retain information. Therefore, a peaceful environment is crucial for providing quality education.



e. According to the speaker, what are the main problems faced by both men and women?

=> According to the speaker, poverty, ignorance, injustice, racism and deprivation of basic rights are the main problems faced by both men and women.



f. What is Malala calling upon all governments?

=> Malala is urging all governments to provide free education for every child worldwide, take a stand against terrorism and violence, and ensure their safety from cruelty and harm.



g. What is the main message of this speech?

=> The main message of this speech is that education is a powerful tool to solve problems like illiteracy, terrorism, violence, and the violation of human rights. That’s why giving education to every child in the world should be the top priority for all governments.
